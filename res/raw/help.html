Welcome!

The goal of the game is to guess a 4 color secret code chosen by the computer.

After each guess, the computer will give you a hint to tell you how close you are to the correct code.
The hint will contain a red peg for each correct color AND position, and a white peg for each correct color but incorrect position.

For instance: if the secret code is PURPLE GREEN YELLOW ORANGE, and you guessed PURPLE YELLOW RED RED, the computer will give you one red peg for PURPLE (correct color AND position) - and one white peg for YELLOW (color present in the code, but not at the correct position).
Note: the order of the pegs in the hint is not important!

Now that you have this hint, you can try again to guess the secret code. If you find it in less than 12 guesses, you have one the game!

How to play

To play you can either tap (or click with trackball) on an empty hole and then chose a color, or use the picker on top of the board, and drag and drop to the hole.
When you have chosen the 4 colors of your guess, press the 'OK' button.


Good luck, code breaker!
